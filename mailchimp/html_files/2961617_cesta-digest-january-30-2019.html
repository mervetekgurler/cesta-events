Workshop talk by Dr. Luca Scholz:
Verticality in Spatial History

January 31 at 5pm
Stanford Archaeology Center

What can vertical perspectives add to spatial history?...Drawing on ongoing research on the use of digital elevation models and on the emergence of early modern “air law”, this talk will discuss the interest and limitations of vertical approaches to early modern spatial history.
