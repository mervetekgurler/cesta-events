Welcome Zheng!

CESTA is pleased to welcome visiting scholar Zheng Zhang (Université Paris-Saclay) of the ROSETTA project. Zheng is a third year PhD student working on monolingual and cross-linguial word embeddings. His research interests are graph-based word embeddings learning and parallel sentence extraction from comparable corpora. Please stop by the center to learn more about his work and see it online at: https://zzcoolj.github.io. Welcome Zheng!

George Watt Prize, 2019

Students from anywhere in the world are invited to submit an essay or thesis chapter about any aspect of the Spanish Civil War, the global political or cultural struggles against fascism in 1920s and 1930s, or the lifetime histories and contributions of the international volunteers who fought in support of the Spanish Republic from 1936 to 1938. Work will be judged on the basis of originality, quality of research, and effectiveness of argument or presentation.

Submission deadline: July, 1, 2019
