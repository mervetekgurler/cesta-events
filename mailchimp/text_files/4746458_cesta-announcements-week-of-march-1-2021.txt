CESTA News

We are elated to announce that the Modernist Archives Publishing Project (MAPP) (http://www.modernistarchives.com/)  was awarded a UK-US New Directions in Digital Scholarship for Cultural Institutions Grant by the Arts and Humanities Research Council (AHRC) (https://ahrc.ukri.org/) . Kudos to Dr. Alice Staveley (https://english.stanford.edu/people/alice-staveley) and the international team of MAPP collaborators on this major grant. Read more (https://cesta.stanford.edu/news/modernist-archives-publishing-project-receives-major-grant)

Congratulations to Prof. Frederico Freitas (https://history.ncsu.edu/people/faculty_staff/fsantos2) (NC State University) on his forthcoming book, Nationalizing Nature: Iguazu Falls and National Parks at the Brazil-Argentina Border, published by Cambridge University Press (available March 2021). Read more (https://cesta.stanford.edu/news/new-book-dr-frederico-freitas)
