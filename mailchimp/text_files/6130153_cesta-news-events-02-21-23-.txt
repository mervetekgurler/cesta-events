Next Week at CESTA


** CESTA/CIDR Lightning Talks (https://cesta.stanford.edu/events/cestacidr-dh-lightning-talks)
------------------------------------------------------------
Tuesday, April 25  | 12:00 -1:15 | Wallenberg 433A

CESTA and CIDR will once again be co-hosting our annual session of digital humanities lightning talks on Tuesday, April 25, 12:00-1:15pm, to be held in-person at CESTA (a Zoom link is available upon request for remote attendance). The format is casual, with short 3-5 minute lightning talks followed by discussion and feedback from the group.

Anyone with an interest in digital humanities is invited to attend and hear about exciting on-going dh research happening at Stanford.
