Open House for Graduate Students (lunch included!)

"DH and Graduate Studies at Stanford and Beyond" (https://cesta.stanford.edu/events/cesta-open-house-dh-graduate-studies-stanford-beyond)

October 11 | 12-1.15 PM | Wallenberg Hall, Room 433A
About this event:  CESTA cordially invites all graduate students to an open house at our workspace in Wallenberg Hall. All graduate students are welcome, irrespective of year, discipline, knowledge of the digital humanities, or technical background. Participants will learn more about CESTA’s support for the digital humanities and the Center’s unique opportunities for graduate students, including the Graduate Certificate in Digital Humanities (https://cesta.stanford.edu/graduate-programs/graduate-certificate-digital-humanities) and Digital Humanities Graduate Fellowship (https://cesta.stanford.edu/graduate-programs/digital-humanities-graduate-fellowship) . Grads will have an opportunity to meet peers and raise questions with Mark Algee-Hewitt (https://english.stanford.edu/people/mark-algee-hewitt) , Associate Professor of English and Director of the Graduate Certificate, Will Fenton
(https://profiles.stanford.edu/william-fenton#:~:text=Will%20Fenton%20is%20Associate%20Director,literature%20and%20the%20digital%20humanities.) , Associate Director of CESTA and Director of the Graduate Fellowship Program, and Alix Keener (https://cesta.stanford.edu/people/alix-keener) , Digital Scholarship Coordinator at CESTA.

About this series: Every Tuesday lunchtime (12-1.15 PM) during the academic quarter, CESTA hosts events on topics relevant to the digital humanities. For information about upcoming events in this series, visit our website (https://cesta.stanford.edu/events/cesta-seminar-series) .
