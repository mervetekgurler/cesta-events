** Research Opportunities for
Stanford Undergrads
------------------------------------------------------------


CESTA's Research Internship Program provides opportunities for Stanford undergraduate students to collaborate on faculty-led projects throughout the academic year. Through structured research training, experimentation with cutting-edge technologies, and mentorship, Research Interns develop valuable academic and professional skills. Applications for Winter, Spring, and Summer 2021 positions are now being accepted.

View the Full Announcement and Apply (https://cesta.stanford.edu/news/undergraduate-research-opportunities-cesta-winter-spring-andor-summer-2021)
Application Deadline and Timeline
* Monday, January 6, 2021 (by 11:59pm): regular application deadline (https://forms.gle/caKUgK8Bt4ToWRvbA) .
* Week of January 11: Group Interviews over Zoom; offer letters sent to students for all quarters.
* Weeks of January 18: Orientation and initial project meetings for students working in the Winter quarter.

Current CESTA Projects Include:
* Analyzing historical evictions and dispossession across 30 cities globally to determine patterns of urbanization and forced relocation
* Applying computational modeling to reveal stylistic nuance and social bias in thousands of texts
* Developing online portals for newly digitized historical archives, creating innovative teaching and research opportunities
* Predicting the future of human communication by analyzing historical communication technologies
* Studying how the world has changed during the pandemic and gathering stories and art related to life in quarantine

To learn about the recent work of Research Interns, check out the 2019 CESTA Summer Research Anthology (https://cesta.stanford.edu/news/anthology-2019)  and Intern Spotlight videos on CESTA's YouTube channel (https://www.youtube.com/channel/UCok8JHwa7L9XR1FN6bftGyw) . To hear about the CESTA Research Intern experience, contact our CESTA Ambassador (https://cesta.stanford.edu/news/undergraduate-research-opportunities-cesta-winter-spring-andor-summer-2021) .
