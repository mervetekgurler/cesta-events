Announcements and Opportunities

January 13 | 10 AM–3 PM
SSDS Bash Bootcamp (https://library.stanford.edu/events/ssds-bash-bootcamp)

Have you ever wanted to build a basic data science toolkit but can’t get started? No need for Python and R to hog all the credit, kickstart Winter Quarter with SSDS’s Bash Bootcamp!

This 4-hour data science project will boost your confidence through transferrable skills essential for the modern, data-driven job market.

Here you will learn the basics of the: 1) Bash programming language, 2) SQLite database engine, 3) Git for version control, and 4) free static GitHub Pages.
Online, via Zoom. Register here. (https://appointments.library.stanford.edu/event/10071823)

Register for Women in Data Science (WiDS) Conference (https://www.widsconference.org/)

WiDS elevates women in the field by providing inspiration, education, community and support. Join us in-person on 3/8/23 to see outstanding women discussing their work in data science and related fields. Everyone is welcome. Register here. (https://www.eventbrite.com/e/women-in-data-science-wids-stanford-conference-2023-tickets-428829670087?aff=SDSnewsletter)

Workshops on Publishing in DHQ

Starting in January 2023, Digital Humanities Quarterly (http://www.digitalhumanities.org/dhq/) 's peer review team will be holding a series of online workshops to provide information for potential authors about DHQ's submission, review and publication process. The workshop is aimed at authors new to digital humanities. We're starting with two pilot events:
January 12, 8 AM Eastern / 5 AM Pacific
January 13, 1 PM Eastern / 10 AM Pacific
Register here. (https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLSfow_DJNZCHaRPhOCZacTpO-Xbp0i4HXrtzOX5hL1K2ZQgy2w/viewform)

CFP: "Overcoming Divides: Electronic Literature and Social Change"

We invite proposals for papers and panels for the 2023 Electronic Literature Organization Conference, which will take place in Coimbra, Portugal on July 12-15. Access the full CFP and submission instructions (https://eliterature.org/2022/11/cfp-elo-2023-coimbra-jan-20-july-12-15-2023/) . Deadline is Jan 20.
