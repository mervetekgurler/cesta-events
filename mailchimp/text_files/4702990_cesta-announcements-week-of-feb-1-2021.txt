Upcoming Online Events
Week of February 1, 2021
In the context of Stanford's policy concerning the COVID-19 pandemic, CESTA events are online for now. We look forward to seeing you at CESTA when it is safe to gather again.

Our new transnational seminar series The Digital Humanities Long View, (https://cestastanford.github.io/) hosted in collaboration with UCL Centre for Digital Humanities, will resume on February 9.
