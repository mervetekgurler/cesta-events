** CESTA Digest | Week of September 21
------------------------------------------------------------
This Week

CESTA Seminar
Poetic Media Lab: A Digital Humanities Hub (https://cesta.stanford.edu/events/poetic-media-lab-digital-humanities-hub)
Tuesday, Sept. 22 from 12 - 1:15 pm
Via Zoom. Registration required.
Free & open to the public.

This will be the Poetic Media Lab’s (https://poeticmedia.stanford.edu/) most comprehensive presentation to date, a celebration of the variety of initiatives it sponsors and an opportunity to engage with other educators, creators and researchers.

CIDR Workshop
Intro to R for Humanities and Social Sciences (https://library.stanford.edu/events/introduction-r-digital-tools-and-methods-humanities-and-social-sciences-1)
Tuesday, September 22, 1 - 3 pm

Via Zoom. Limited to Stanford affiliates. Registration Required.

This introductory workshop is geared towards those who would like to get started with R or need to brush up on their skills.
Rumsey Map Center Event
Data Visualization and the Modern Imagination: Virtual Exhibition Opening (https://us14.campaign-archive.com/?u=bc280d099b8c85ba9fd6e0bb0&id=c0cced8586)
Friday, September 25, 2020 at 3:00 pm. Link to Register (https://stanford.zoom.us/webinar/register/WN_WynKLLYcTXuX6gdHmp3kuQ)

For Stanford Graduate Students
Digital Humanities Grad Fellowship (https://cesta.stanford.edu/dhgf)

The application is open to Stanford graduate students working on individual projects or collaborative projects with faculty. Priority is given to PhD students in the arts and humanities who have completed their required coursework or are TGR. Applications are due by October 6.
