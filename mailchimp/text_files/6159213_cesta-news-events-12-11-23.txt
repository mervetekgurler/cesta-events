Meet the 2023-24 Cohort of Digital Humanities Research Fellows (https://cesta.stanford.edu/people/dh-fellows)

Every year, CESTA welcomes a new cohort of digital humanities fellows, who each pursue their own research projects. This year, CESTA will welcomes seven fellows representing a diversity of departments and research interests.
