** Research Opportunities for
Stanford Undergrads
------------------------------------------------------------

CESTA's Research Internship Program provides opportunities for Stanford undergraduate students to collaborate on faculty-led projects throughout the academic year. Through structured research training, experimentation with cutting-edge technologies, and mentorship, Research Interns develop valuable academic and professional skill sets. Applications for Winter-Spring 2020 and Summer 2020 positions are now being accepted.
View the Full Announcement and Apply (https://cesta.stanford.edu/news/undergraduate-research-winter-spring-summer-2020)


** Application Deadline and Timeline
------------------------------------------------------------
* Sunday, December 8, 2019: early applications considered (https://docs.google.com/forms/d/1EuwdwKY_zvHGpAtUh6BxFrUgD7i4Mf1-TKqhcJpX5GI/edit)
* Monday, January 13, 2020 (by 11:59pm): regular application deadline (https://docs.google.com/forms/d/1EuwdwKY_zvHGpAtUh6BxFrUgD7i4Mf1-TKqhcJpX5GI/edit)
* Week of January 13: Group Interviews at CESTA
* Weeks of January 20 and 27: Offers for Winter-Spring 2020 positions emailed. (Summer offers will be confirmed later.)


** Current CESTA Projects include:
------------------------------------------------------------
* Analyzing historical evictions and dispossession across 30 cities globally to determine patterns of urbanization and forced relocation
* Applying computational modeling to reveal stylistic nuance and social bias in thousands of texts
* Developing online portals for newly digitized historical archives, creating innovative teaching and research opportunities
* Predicting the future of human communication by analyzing historical communication technologies

To learn about the recent work of Research Interns, check out the 2019 CESTA Summer Research Anthology (https://cesta.stanford.edu/news/anthology-2019)  and Intern Spotlight videos on CESTA's YouTube channel (https://www.youtube.com/channel/UCok8JHwa7L9XR1FN6bftGyw) .
