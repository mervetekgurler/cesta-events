Venture: Humanities & Sciences Opportunities

Date
Thu January 25th 2018, 6:00pm
Event Sponsor
BEAM, Stanford Career Education
Location
Ng House Student Lounge
Not sure yet what you're doing with your summer or after you graduate? You are not alone! Connect with hand-picked employers wishing to recruit H & S students plus organizations from across campus who have summer internships, research opportunities, and fellowships available both in the U.S. and abroad.
 
To see a list of registered employers and organizations go to:  https://app.joinhandshake.com/career_fairs/3745/employers_list
 
Need some prep help?  Join us for Coaches Corner where our talented Humanities and Sciences Coaches will help you prepare to get the most out of the event.  Learn networking tips from the coaches and from each other, and get questions answered before the Venture: Humanities &Sciences Opportunities event kicks off.  Group sessions will start at 5:00 and 5:30 on the lower level of Ng House.
